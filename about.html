About Heritage Auctions & Antiques

Heritage Auctions & Antiques has been helping collectors buy and sell rare items for over 25 years.

Our history

Founded by a family of dealers in London, we began with fine clocks and silver and now handle collections spanning furniture, ceramics, jewelry, and decorative arts.

Our specialists

Each lot is reviewed by category experts with market and provenance knowledge to support transparent pricing.
